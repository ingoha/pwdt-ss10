New Sites
St.Kitts, Aug 2. X.Presso, the CEO of JKAM, Inc. announced yesterday that a contract had been signed with the Canoo group with regard to the JKA application. The canoo group is a group of university hospitals located in the western part of Ontario, Canada. A spokesperson for the Canoo group, mrs. Sandra Fleming, said that "the JKA application, when compared to programs that have a similair functionality, clearly is a leader in its field." 		Interfacing JKA
After consultation with the Royal St.Kitts Association of Liquid Food Controllers JKAM, Inc. has developed a new version of the JKA-C module. This module takes care of the information exchange between JKA and third-party applications. Developers can contact mrs. Anne Beethoven on extension 4856 for the latest information.

JKA Announces third quarter profits
Press ReleaseJava Kitchen Managament Applications of St.Kitts (NYSE: JKMA) today announced a record 3rd quarter profit of KD 1,200,000. This is a 230% increase over the same period last year. X.Presso, CEO of JKMA, stated that 'the growth of the company is based on the quelity of the product, which is recognized by our existing as well as our new customers.'. The profits are expected to exceed KD 5,000,000 this fiscal year. 'We recently signed a contract with a large number of Canadian hospitals, which was a new market for us, an we aim to be a global player in this market within the next 2 years', said X.Presso. Contact mrs. Anne Beethoven of JKMA for more information.

JKA Announces merger with Coffee, Inc.
Press ReleaseJava Kitchen Managament Applications of St.Kitts (NYSE: JKMA) today announced its merger with Coffee, Inc. of Marlborough county IN. 'The long term strategem of both companies is to be a leading vendor or dietary and food management applications' according to X.Presso, its CEO. JKMA is the major vendor of the kitchen managment application JKA. Contact mrs. Anne Beethoven of JKMA for more information.
